Monetization Step by Step

    • Sabse pehle ek Google account (Gmail) bana lijiye agar pehle se nahi hai.
    • YouTube par login karein, profile icon par click karke "Create a channel" select karein.
    • Apne channel ka naam, profile picture (logo) aur banner set karein.
    • Description mein likhein ki aapka channel kis topic par videos banata hai (niche).
    • About section mein contact email aur social links (Instagram, etc.) add karein.

  2. Monetization se pehle zaroori hai ki aapka content strong ho:

    • Ek niche select karein – jaise education, tech, gaming, vlog, cooking, motivation, etc.
    • Aisi cheez choose karein jismein aapko knowledge + interest dono ho.
    • Har week kam se kam 2–3 videos daalne ki koshish karein.
    • Audio clear rakhein, video thoda simple ho to bhi chalega, lekin awaz samajh mein aani chahiye.
    • Thumbnail aur title ko attractive banayein, lekin clickbait na karein.

  3. Current basic requirements (approximate – YouTube kabhi-kabhi update karta rehta hai):

    • Channel par 1000 subscribers (subs).
    • Pichle 12 mahine mein kam se kam 4000 public watch hours (normal videos ke liye),
      ya 10 million (1 crore) valid public Shorts views (90 din ke andar) – YouTube region ke hisaab se thoda change ho sakta hai.
    • YouTube ke Community Guidelines aur Copyright rules follow hone chahiye (no strikes).
    • Channel par 2-step verification (phone + email) security on honi chahiye.

    Dhyan rakhein: Requirements time ke saath change bhi ho sakti hain. Exact numbers ke liye hamesha YouTube Studio ke Monetization tab ko check karein.

    1. Desktop par studio.youtube.com open karein (mobile par browser ya app se bhi ja sakte hain).
    2. Left side menu se "Monetization" par click karein.
    3. Yaha par aapko subscriber count aur watch hours / Shorts views ka live progress dikhega.
    4. Jab aapka channel requirements ke paas hoga, YouTube aapko notification bhi bhejega.
    1. Monetization tab mein jaakar "Apply" button par click karein (jab wo active ho jaye).
    2. YouTube aapko kuch terms & conditions dikhayega – unko dhyaan se padhein aur accept karein.
    3. Phir aapse AdSense account link karne ko kaha jayega (agar nahi hai to naya banana hoga).
    4. Apply karne ke baad YouTube aapke channel ko review karega. Ye process kuch din se lekar kuch hafton tak ho sakta hai.

    Is dauraan aap normal tarike se videos dalte rahein. Review ke baad aapko mail / notification aayega ke aapka channel approve hua ya nahi.

    • AdSense ek Google ka advertising platform hai jahan YouTube ka payment aata hai.
    • Monetization apply process ke time YouTube aapko automatically AdSense banane / link karne ka option dega.
    • AdSense banate waqt apna legal naam, address, phone number bilkul sahi-sahi dalein.
    • Address verify karne ke liye kabhi-kabhi PIN code aapke ghar par post se aata hai – use AdSense mein enter karna hota hai.
    • Phir AdSense mein apna bank account add karein jahan par YouTube ki earning transfer hogi.

    2. Ek video select karein, upar monetization ka "$" icon dikhai dega – uspe click karein.
    3. Yaha se aap Monetization: On kar sakte hain aur kis type ke ads dikhne chahiye wo select kar sakte hain (skippable, non-skippable, etc.).
    4. Long videos (8 minutes se zyada) mein mid-roll ads bhi laga sakte hain.

    Naye videos upload karte waqt bhi aap upload process ke time hi monetization on kar sakte hain.

    • Copyright: Bina permission ke gaane, movies, web series, kisi aur ka content use na karein.
    • Reused content: Sirf doosre ka video download karke re-upload mat karein. Apna original content ya proper commentary / transformation add karein.
    • Community Guidelines: Violence, hate speech, sexual content, harassing content se bachein.
    • Agar strike aata hai to usko seriously lein – repeat karne se channel ki monetization band ho sakti hai.
    • CPM (Cost Per Mille): 1000 ad impressions ka rate – advertisers kitna pay kar rahe hain.
    • RPM (Revenue Per Mille): 1000 views par creator ko kitna paisa mil raha hai (YouTube ka hissa nikal kar).
    • Threshold: AdSense ka biasanya $100 (lagbhag ₹7,000–₹8,000) threshold hota hai – isse kam hone par payment release nahi hota.
    • Earnings country, niche, video type, season (festive, Q4) ke hisaab se bahut zyada up-down ho sakti hain.

  10. Jab channel grow hone lage to aap in options se extra earning kar sakte hain:

    • Brand Sponsorships – companies aapko apne product / service promote karne ke paise deti hain.
    • Affiliate Marketing – kisi product ka link description mein dete hain, sale hone par commission milta hai.
    • Own Products / Courses – apna digital course, ebook, merch, coaching, etc. bech sakte hain.
    • Channel Memberships & Super Chat – loyal audience aapko directly support kar sakti hai.

Short Summary (Quick Revision)

  1. Channel banayein, basic setup complete karein.
  2. Niche select karke regular, quality content banayein.
  3. 1000 subscribers + 4000 watch hours (ya Shorts requirement) complete karein.
  4. YouTube Studio ke Monetization tab se YPP ke liye apply karein.
  5. AdSense account banakar link karein, address & bank verify karein.
  6. Channel approve hone ke baad videos par monetization (ads) on karein.
  7. Policies follow karein, copyright / strikes se bachein.
  8. Earnings ko samjhein aur dheere-dheere multiple income sources banayein.

Note: YouTube ki policies aur requirements time‑time par change ho sakti hain. Hamesha official YouTube Help Center aur YouTube Studio ke Monetization page par latest information zarur check karein.
